About Us

Cyberland are a family owned and managed Theme Park in the South East of England. Over the years we have grown from a small park with only 12 employees to a nationally recognised place to spend the day attracting over 230,000 visitors every year! We have a reputation for delivering quality entertainment in a safe environment for all ages. We sincerely believe in our motto “Where the fun never stops!”
